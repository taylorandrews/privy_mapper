About Reveal Estate

My Project - The Basics

The goal of my capstone project - Reveal Estate - is to algorithmically group houses into 'nests' of relevant comps. By taking the purely geographically based zones that Privy already made, and finding the patterns that they used, my algorithm is able to categorize new data. It's even capable of getting data from a totally new city, with different streets and features and create house nests from scratch.
